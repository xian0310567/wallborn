## Asset Intake Rules

1. Record every external asset before using it in scenes/scripts.
2. Prefer CC0/free assets for first prototype passes.
3. For paid assets, record purchase source and license before importing.
4. Avoid mixing packs if pixel size, palette, camera angle, or outline style conflicts.
5. Do not commit assets with unclear commercial-use or redistribution rights.
